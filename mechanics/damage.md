## Overview

Damage in Darkhaven is calculated based on several factors including base damage, scaling attributes, and damage modifiers.

## Damage Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Physical | Raw physical force from weapons and attacks |
| Fire | Burning damage that may ignite enemies |
| Cold | Freezing damage that may slow or chill |
| Lightning | Shock damage that may chain between targets |
| Chaos | Unstable damage that bypasses some resistances |
